App conceptual summary : Your project is a private reading companion designed entirely for your own use. It lets you build a personal library, read books, track your progress, and maintain a complete history of your reading activity. Over time, it learns your habits, identifies patterns in how and when you read, measures your consistency and progress, and turns that information into meaningful insights. Rather than simply storing books, it acts like a personal reading coach by helping you understand your behavior, estimate future progress, stay motivated through goals and achievements, and discover what you may want to read next. The overall experience is focused less on managing books and more on understanding, improving, and enjoying your life as a reader.
